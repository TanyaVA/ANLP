Our Pastor

Keith D. Brooks has been serving as Senior Pastor of All Nations Life and Praise (formerly All Nations Church of God) since 2001, where he has been a member all of his life. Under his leadership, All Nations has experienced tremendous spiritual and physical growth.

Pastor Brooks was licensed and ordained under the headship of the late Bishop George Hoke, whom he succeeded as Pastor after his death in 2000. Having tall shoes to fill, Pastor Brooks has very capably carried on the legacy and teachings that his congregation was accustom to. He preaches the Word with vigor, fire, and conviction. Bringing Change, Restoration & A Message of Hope to the World, Pastor Keith D. Brooks is known as the preacher with "much energy". He is also a gifted organizer and administrator, which has allowed him to be instrumental in taking All Nations to higher heights.

As a young man of God, he serves as a mentor to many young people. This was evident even before he became Pastor while serving as a Youth Minister for many years. He has led many young men and women to Christ just by the example of holiness that he exemplifies.

Pastor Brooks was educated in the Dekalb County School System, graduating from Southwest Dekalb High School. He also attended Dekalb Technical Institute. He is retired from the Department of Family and Children Services.

In 1990, Pastor Brooks and his brother, Kendrick, organized the gospel group, "Keith Brooks and New Creation". This music ministry encompassedÂ a group of gifted singers and musicians, who not only performed musically, but ministers to the hearts of many under the anointing of God. Under the vision of Pastor Brooks, these ministers traveled throughout the United States and around the world, proclaiming the good news of Jesus Christ. The ministrels were blessed with the recordings of two studio projects, "Everlasting Love" and "Perfect Peace".

The sole and priority purpose of Pastor Brooks and the All Nations Life & Praise Ministry is to transform and change lives through the power of the Holy Ghost where they are committed to developing and training men and women to fulfill their purpose and destiny in Christ Jesus.
